About This Tool

This tool is designed to help users practice solving the RE2R medallion puzzles in Resident Evil 2 Remake. It provides an interactive interface to simulate the puzzle-solving experience, with a timer, so you can practice and improve to save time during your runs. I have also tried to simulate the input lag between each keypress to better reflect the delay experienced in-game. I plan to add sounds and more visual effects in the future to enhance the experience, with the main goal being to maintain performance and responsiveness. As I am a beginner runner, I am yet to add the B scenario medallion puzzles, but they will be added as soon as I learn them myself.

Controls

  • Use W, S or the Up and Down arrow keys to change symbols.
  • Use A, D or the Left and Right arrow keys to change positions of the symbols.
  • Press Space, E, F, Q or Enter keys to submit.
  • Press the R key to reset the puzzle and timer.
